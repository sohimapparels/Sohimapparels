Services

Our Services

CUTTING

Our production process begins with fabric cutting — a vital step that ensures every garment is made to exact specifications. Using both manual and automated methods, we cut fabric with precision to reduce waste and maintain consistency. This attention to detail lays the foundation for high-quality stitching and flawless final products.

STITCHING

Our skilled team ensures every garment is stitched with accuracy and care. Using advanced machines and quality threads, we maintain strong seams, neat finishes, and consistent sizing—delivering comfort, durability, and style in every piece.

THREAD CUTTING

After stitching, each garment goes through careful thread cutting to remove excess threads and ensure a neat, polished look. This final touch enhances the overall presentation and meets our high-quality standards before moving to the finishing stage.

PRESSING

Pressing is the final step that gives each garment a crisp, clean look. Using industrial steam press machines, we smooth out wrinkles, set shapes, and enhance the overall presentation—ensuring every piece is ready for packing and delivery.

CHECKING

Every garment undergoes a thorough quality check to ensure it meets our high standards. We inspect for stitching accuracy, measurements, fabric defects, and overall finishing—delivering only the best to our customers.

PACKAGING

After final checks, each garment is carefully folded, tagged, and packed to ensure it stays clean and presentable during transit. We use secure, eco-friendly packaging that protects the product and reflects our commitment to quality.
